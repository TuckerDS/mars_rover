Mars Rover

INSTRUCTIONS:
  • Landing: Press button to deploy your rover at random position and direction."
  • AutoRover: Press button to deploy your rover at random position and direction and a second rover with random movements."
  • Move: Use cursors to move, turn left, turn right, go forward and go backward."
  • Run Commands: Write your movements commands and press button to run.
  • Commands: Example commands: FFLFFRBRL
    "F" Forward
    "B" Backward
    "R" Turn right
    "L" Turn left
